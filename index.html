How it works

Dreams creates infinite generative chord progressions by seamlessly crossfading between 21 different chord textures. Click any chord below to add it to your queue, or let the algorithm select randomly.

When the queue is empty, the algorithm intelligently selects chords based on weighted randomness - songs you've heard recently are less likely to repeat.
