How it works

Dreams creates infinite generative chord progressions by seamlessly crossfading between 21 different chord textures. Each song is 37 seconds long with 7-second crossfades creating smooth, meditative transitions.

The algorithm intelligently selects the next chord based on weighted randomness - songs you've heard recently are less likely to repeat, but never impossible.
